about

Hey! My name is Aarnav Munji and I'm the current TSA Region 11 Treasurer. I'm a high school student in Texas passionate about using STEM and technology to solve problems. As regional treasurer, I've planned and hosted many events, including banquets, leadership conferences, service drives, and other community initiatives to increase opportunities for students to connect with technology. Along with this, I've also recruited more middle schools to join our growing chapter.

Outside of TSA, I'm interested in finding ways to make technology and education more accessible, whether it's through robotics, hackathons or hosting my school's computer science club. I strongly believe that technology should be used to help people in their everyday lives, which is why I enjoy turning ideas and prototypes into practical applications.

This website highlights my leadership experience, projects, and initiatives. It also serves a place where I can share what I'm learning as I continue to grow in different fields including engineering, computer science, and leadership.

plans

my platform:

As Texas TSA State Treasurer, my goal is to ensure that every student has the opportunity to explore technology, develop their talent, and showcase their work. I also want to increase the accessibility and visibility of TSA for students who are currently unable to participate.

increasing student opportunities

What does this look like?

  • expanding internship and industry partnership opportunities for TSA members
  • improving travel sponsorships so that students can attend and compete at conferences
  • developing more scholarships and financial aid programs for TSA members
  • create state-wide hackathons and other competitions for students to gain recognition

making TSA more accessible and visible

What does this look like?

  • improving outreach and information spread to schools or districts that don't have chapters
  • creating "TSA Starter Kits" with basic supplies and guidance to help students start projects
  • organizing general fundraisers for chapters facing financial challenges
  • strengthen collaboration between the chapter, regional, and state levels to share resources better

credibility:

As a Regional Treasurer and former chapter president, I've led sponsorship efforts, fundraising initiatives, and chapter support across multiple schools. I've also helped develop an internship program at the regional level to build more opportunities for students. These plans build directly on existing TSA structures and focus on scalable, achievable improvements.
